 Jabberwocky, cried Vice President Pence,
“We must put a stop to this nonsense!
How can we protect our young from the scourge
Of a virus with no vaccination?

“Confound this Harvard,” he ranted and raged;
“What they really are after is cash!
For they know, as they’re spoon-feeding the news,
That the best way to immunize is with a shot.”
